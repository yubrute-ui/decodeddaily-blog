We do not directly collect personal information from visitors. This site may use standard analytics and advertising technologies that collect information automatically, including:

This site may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks, including **Google AdSense**. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

- You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).
- You can opt out of some third-party vendor use of cookies by visiting the [Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page](https://optout.aboutads.info/).

Posts may link to third-party sites (for example, YouTube or source articles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

## Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
