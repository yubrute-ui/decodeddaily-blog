**DecodedDaily** breaks down one cognitive bias or mental shortcut at a time — the hidden psychology running your everyday decisions.

Every post pairs a real, established psychological concept with a concrete example, so you walk away with something you can actually notice in your own life: your own thinking, someone else's argument, or a headline you just scrolled past.

Short video versions of each post are published daily on [YouTube](https://youtube.com/@DecodedDailyWeekly), TikTok, and Instagram.

## What this site is not

DecodedDaily is an educational and commentary site about psychology and decision-making. It is not therapy, medical advice, or a substitute for professional guidance. If something here resonates and you want to go deeper, talk to a qualified professional.
